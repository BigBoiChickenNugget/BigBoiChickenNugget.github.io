Wheelchair Phone Charger

For my freshman year final engineering design project at McMaster University, I worked in a team of 4 individuals to design and create a design that would help a client assigned to us by the university. Our client was a young individual diagnosed with spina bifida, a condition that inhibits the development of the spinal cord and causes paralysis in the lower body. Our client used a wheelchair to move around and mentioned that they had difficulty charging their phone due to the location of charging ports.
